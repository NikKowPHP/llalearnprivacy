Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our language learning social network application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes information that can be used to identify you, such as your name, email address, profile picture, and language learning goals. You provide this information when you create an account and use the App.
  • Language Learning Data: This includes information related to your language learning progress, such as completed lessons, flashcards, vocabulary lists, and test scores. This data is collected both locally on your device and in our Firebase databases.
  • Usage Data: This includes information about how you use the App, such as the features you access, the time you spend on the App, and your interactions with other users. This data is collected through Firebase analytics and may be linked to your personal information.
  • Device Information: This includes information about your device, such as the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers. This information is collected automatically when you use the App.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the App's features and functionality.
  • To personalize your learning experience and track your progress.
  • To connect you with other language learners and facilitate social interactions.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App's performance.
  • To send you notifications and updates about the App.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Data Storage and Security

We store your personal information and language learning data securely in Firebase databases. We also use local storage on your device to store your progress and preferences. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Data Sharing

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the App, such as Firebase and analytics providers. We may also share your information with other users of the App, as necessary to facilitate social interactions and language learning activities. We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your Choices

You can access, update, or delete your personal information by logging into your account settings. You can also choose to disable certain features of the App, such as notifications and location services. You can opt-out of data collection by uninstalling the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and notify you through the App.
